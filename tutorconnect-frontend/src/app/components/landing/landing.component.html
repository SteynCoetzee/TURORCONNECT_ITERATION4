Meet the Head of Innovation

Kwanda Camane

Data Engineer & Founder

Smith's Tutoring is a world of academic consultants with industry experience and a deep love for academia. We provide tutoring and tailored services to students across the EMS, EBIT, BSC, Mathematics and Engineering Faculties.

Our services extend beyond the University of Pretoria — we have proudly rendered services to students at Stellenbosch University, the University of Cape Town, and the University of the Witwatersrand.

Kwanda carried the company alone for 4 years with passion and dedication. As demand grew and collaborators joined, Smith's Tutoring expanded into the thriving academic consultancy it is today.
